Biography

About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in Information Systems at the University of Texas at Arlington. I received my PhD in Information Systems from the University of Maryland. My research focuses on developing and applying deep learning methods to infer consumer behavior and make better business decisions from unstructured data. I am especially interested in computer vision and multimodal learning.
